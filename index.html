About

Melina Axelrad is an artist whose work explores the intersection of memory, emotion, and landscape. Through her paintings, she creates spaces that feel both familiar and open, inviting viewers to reflect on their own experiences and transitions.

Her work combines acrylic, graphite, and metallic paints to create pieces that offer moments of grounding and contemplation in an ever-changing world.
